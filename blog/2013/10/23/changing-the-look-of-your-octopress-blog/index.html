Changing the Look of Your Octopress Blog.

Oct 23rd, 2013

So, once you make your Octopress blog, there are a ton of themes to choose from. If you’d like to change your theme, you can find a ton by doing a simple Google search.

3rd Party Octopress Themes

For example, I installed the Minimalist theme by doing the following —

cd octopress
git clone git://github.com/ryandeussing/octopress-minimalist.git .themes/minimalist
rake install['minimalist']
rake generate

Then refer to the index.html and _config.yml files to set up your site (change links, add title, and so on.)
